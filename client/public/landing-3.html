Questions

Good to know.

Do parents need to install an app?

No app store needed. VidyaSetu opens in any browser and can be added to the home screen, where it feels and works just like a native app, offline-friendly and full-screen.

Can I move my existing student data in?

Yes. Add students in minutes or import them in bulk. Most academies are fully live the same day they sign up.

How do fee reminders work?

VidyaSetu knows who owes what and sends gentle reminders on a schedule you set, with a UPI QR code for one-tap payment, so collection stays on autopilot.

Is my data safe?

Each academy's data is isolated and backed up automatically every day, so you keep every record.
