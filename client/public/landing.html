Questions, answered

Everything you might want to know before getting started.

Do parents and teachers need to download anything?

No. VidyaSetu opens in any browser. For an app-like experience, both teachers and parents can "Add to Home Screen" to install it as a PWA — no Play Store or App Store needed.

How are notifications sent?

Two ways: an in-app notification bell inside the portal, and web-push notifications that reach the phone's lock screen even when the app is closed — for class reminders, fee reminders, new assignments and more.

Is my academy's data private?

Yes. Each academy's data is fully isolated, and every parent can only ever see information for their own child. Logins are secure and role-based.

What's the difference between Core and Complete?

Core (₹50/student) covers the day-to-day essentials: students, attendance, timetable, fees, messaging and the parent portal. Complete (₹100/student) adds online video lessons, quizzes & certificates, assignments, question papers and reports.

Can I import my existing student list?

Absolutely. You can bulk-import students from a spreadsheet to get set up in minutes, then create batches and your weekly timetable.
